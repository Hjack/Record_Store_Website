Jazz

"Jazz is the big brother of Revolution. Revolution follows it around."- Miles Davis

Whether you are into the bepob of the 1940's, fusion of the late 1960's or soul jazz of the 1970's, Leroy's Records has the deepest jazz collection in the Bay Area. There are rows of vinyl from labesls such as Flying Dutchman, MPS, Black Jazz, Impulse!, Blue Note, Strata-East, ECM, Milestone, Prestige, CTI, Verve, Atlantic and much more.

If you are new to jazz and need some direction, here's a list of Ahmad's essential jazz records (in no particular order...)

  • Miles Davis- Kind of Blue (Blue Note 1959)
  • Eric Dolphy- Outward Bound (New Jazz 1960)
  • John Coltrane- Giant Steps (Atlantic 1960)
  • Bill Evans Trio- Explorations (Riverside 1961)
  • Wayne Shorter- Speak No Evil (Blue Note 1965)
