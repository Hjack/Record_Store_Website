Funk

"Put a glide in your stride, a dip in yo' hip and come on up to the mothersip."- George Clinton

Oakland is known for funk. After all, the Bay is known for giving the world Sly & The Family Stone. Leroy's has a DEEP collection of funk. Sly, Parliament-Funkadelic, James Brown, The Meters, Bootsy....we got it all

If you are new to funk and need some direction, here's a list of essential funk records (in no particular order...)

  • Parliament- Mothership Connection (Casablanca 1975)
  • Funkadelic- One Nation Under a Groove (Warner Bros 1978)
  • Zapp- Zapp (Warner Bros 1980)
  • James Brown- The Payback (Polydor 1974)
  • The Meters- The Meters (Josie 1969)
